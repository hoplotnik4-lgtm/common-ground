Passion

Is the care behind every detail. It shows in the craft, creativity, and love placed into each product and experience.

Balance

Means harmony between coffee, flowers, and baking. It creates a space that feels calm, warm, and alive at the same time.

Community

Is about bringing people together. Common Ground is a place where people can connect, share moments, and feel at home.

Service

Is warm, thoughtful, and attentive. It means making each person feel welcomed, valued, and cared for.

Each city brings its own blossom

The brand territory is the everyday bloom — a place where simple moments begin to flower. Each café feels connected to its city and its local flower.

Milano
Magnolia

Milan is a city of quiet elegance — precise, structured, and deeply refined. The magnolia blooms like soft silk against stone streets, design studios, and timeless architecture, where every detail feels composed: coffee, bread, and flowers arranged with grace.

A magnolia morning in Milan — soft petals, warm coffee, quiet design.

Bouquet — pink magnolia, ranunculus, sweet peas, and delicate seasonal greenery.

Paris
Iris

Paris is a city of art, romance, and lingering moments. The iris unfolds like a painted memory, carrying the softness of poetry, perfume, and morning light — a place for slow conversations, delicate pastries, and coffee rituals that feel intimate, graceful, and timeless.

Paris unfolds like an iris — a coffee made for lingering.

Bouquet — iris, garden roses, lisianthus, and touches of soft foliage.

London
Rosebay

London is a city of grey skies, rain, movement, and unexpected warmth. The rosebay blooms with resilience, bringing colour to brick, fog, and weather — a shelter in the city, where coffee warms the hands, bread softens the day, and flowers bloom through the rain.

A rosebay in the rain — where grey skies bloom warm.

Bouquet — rosebay, scabiosa, waxflower, and loose meadow greenery.

Amsterdam
Tulip

Amsterdam is a city of canals, bicycles, open skies, and bright colour. The tulip rises with simplicity and joy, bringing freshness to the city's rhythm — a bright pause beside the water, where coffee, bread, and flowers feel light, alive, and full of morning.

Amsterdam opens in tulip colour — a bright bloom beside the water.

Bouquet — tulips, hyacinths, anemones, and bright seasonal foliage.

Manifesto

The everyday bloom

We believe every city has a flower — a bloom that carries its rhythm, its weather, its light, its quiet beauty. We believe coffee, bread, and flowers are not separate things, but small rituals born from the same earth.

Coffee warms the hands. Bread gathers people around the table. Flowers carry feelings home.

From Milan to Paris, from London to Amsterdam, every city blooms in its own way.

This is Common Ground. The everyday bloom.
