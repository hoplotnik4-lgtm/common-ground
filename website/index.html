Each city brings its own blossom

You can visit us in four different cities. Each café feels connected to its city and its local flower.

Milano
Magnolia
tap to read

Milan is a city of quiet elegance. It is precise, structured, and refined. The magnolia blooms against the stone streets, design studios, and timeless architecture, where every detail feels composed: coffee, bread, and flowers arranged with grace.

A magnolia morning in Milan

Paris
Iris
tap to read

Paris is a city of art, romance, and lingering moments. The iris unfolds like a painted memory, carrying the softness of poetry, perfume, and morning light. Wake up with slow conversations, delicate pastries, and make coffee a ritual that feels intimate and timeless.

Paris unfolds like an iris

London
Rosebay
tap to read

London is a city of grey skies, rain, movement, warmth and coziness. The rosebay blooms with resilience, bringing colour to brick, fog, and gloomy weather. Take shelter from city, let coffee warm your hands, bread soften the day, and flowers bloom through the rain.

A rosebay in the London rain

Amsterdam
Tulip
tap to read

Amsterdam is a city of canals, bicycles, open skies, and bright colours. The tulip is bringing freshness to the city's rhythm with its purity and simplicity. Pause beside the water, where coffee, bread, and flowers feel light and alive.

Pure like an Amsterdam tulip

Manifesto

The everyday bloom

We believe every city has a flower — a bloom that carries its rhythm, its weather, its light, its quiet beauty. We believe coffee, bread, and flowers are not separate things, but small rituals born from the same earth.

Coffee warms the hands. Bread gathers people around the table. Flowers carry feelings home.

From Milan to Paris, from London to Amsterdam, every city blooms in its own way.

This is Common Ground. The everyday bloom.
